Chocolate Shake

A rich, creamy chocolate shake blended until smooth and thick, with a deep cocoa flavor and just the right amount of sweetness. It is cold, velvety, and satisfying, striking a perfect balance between indulgent and comforting. Every sip tastes classic and familiar, like a simple treat done exactly right.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chocolate ice cream
  • ½ cup milk
  • 2 tbsp chocolate syrup
  • 1-2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • A pinch of salt
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Add everything to a blender.
  2. Blend until smooth and thick.
  3. Pour into a cold glass.
  4. Whipped cream
  5. Chocolate drizzle
  6. Chocolate shavings or sprinkles
  7. Add any additional toppings
  8. A cherry on top
